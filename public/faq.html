frequently asked questions
What are the categories of Pinkfish Countdown tickets?

General Admission

General Admission (GA) tickets allow entry to the General Admission area on the festival

day only.

Tickets will be available for purchase on any date until the festival sells out.

‍

VIP

VIP ticket holders will enjoy exclusive perks such as designated VIP entrances, access to

VIP space with amenities and exclusive viewing areas of the main stage.

VIP tickets are limited in quantity and will be available on a first-come-first-served basis.

‍

Lava Lounge: The VVIP Experience

The Lava Lounge is the most exclusive way to experience the festival, where the

festival-goers will have the best, unblocked view, space to interact and dance with their

fellow friends and special access to all the facilities.

Is re-entry allowed?

No re-entry policy applies to all attendees, Pinkfish would like to ensure a safe and smoothcountdown experience for everyone!

Is there an age limit?

Entry to this event is restricted to individuals who are 18 years of age or older. Validgovernment-issued photo identification with proof of age must be presented upon entry.

What should we wear?

Our only dress code is "express yourself”! Feel free to wear anything that is comfortable toyou. But make sure you don’t wear anything that is prohibited.

Are outside food & drinks allowed?

Outside food and beverages are usually not allowed, but we have a wide variety of food anddrink vendors to keep you fueled throughout the event.

Are there lockers provided at the venue?

Yes. Lockers will be made available at the venue.

Are there any medical facilities at the event in case of emergencies?

We prioritize your safety and well-being. There will be medical facilities and first aid stations on-site, staffed by trained professionals.

What happens if it rains during the event?

Pinkfish Countdown is a rain or shine event! We highly recommend bringing a raincoat or poncho to stay dry if the weather takes a turn.

Are professional cameras allowed?

We're excited that you want to capture the festival, but unfortunately, we do not permit professional cameras.

How can I stay updated on any event announcements?

Stay informed by following our official website and social media channels
Instagram: @pinkfishfestival
Facebook: Pinkfish Festival

I lost my item at the event, where can I retrieve them?

Kindly reach out to us at info@pinkfishfestival.com / 6012-2655783 . We'll do our best to help you locate your lost item.
