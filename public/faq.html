frequently asked questions
1. Where can I purchase tickets to Pinkfish Music & Arts Festival 2024?

You can get them here.

2. What are the categories of Pinkfish Music & Arts Festival 2024 tickets?

General Admission
‍General Admission (GA) tickets allow entry to the General Admission area on the festival day only. Tickets will be available for purchase on any date until the festival sells out.
‍
‍VIP
‍VIP ticket holders will enjoy exclusive perks such as designated VIP entrances, access to VIP space with amenities and exclusive viewing areas of the main stage. VIP tickets are limited in quantity and will be available on a first-come-first-served basis.

VVIP Lounge
The VVIP Lounge is the most exclusive way to experience the event, where the festival goers will have the best, unblocked view, space to interact and dance with their fellow friends and special access to all the facilities.

Whether you come alone or with a group of friends, we’ve got you covered with our sofa packages or single passes!

3. Are VVIP Single Passes available?

Yes! You may now purchase your VVIP Single Pass here.

4. How can I get to the venue?

Find your way to Pinkfish Music & Arts Festival 2024!
‍
Waze/Google Maps: Sunway Lagoon

Public Transport:
‍- KTM Komuter Train
- Putra LRT
- Sunway City’s Bus Rapid Transit Station 3

For more info: https://sunwaylagoon.com/how-to-get-here

5. Where is the entrance to the event?

GA Ticket Holders
Sunway Lagoon Main Entrance (across Texas Chicken, Sunway Pyramid)

VIP / VVIP Lounge Ticket Holders
Sunway Lagoon Surf Beach Entrance (beside Sunway Resort Hotel)

6. Is re-entry allowed?

No re-entry policy applies to all attendees, Pinkfish would like to ensure a safe and smooth festival experience for everyone!

7. Is there an age limit?

Entry to this event is restricted to individuals who are legally 18 years of age or older. Valid government-issued photo identification with proof of age must be presented upon entry.

8. Where can I retrieve the set time?

Stay updated via our social media channel as we will post ahead of time!
‍
Instagram: @pinkfishfestival  
Facebook: Pinkfish Festival

9. What should we wear?

Our only dress code is "express yourself”! Feel free to wear anything that is comfortable to you. But make sure you don’t wear anything that contains any religious and/or political symbols that carries negative connotations.

10. Are outside food & drinks allowed?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but we have a wide variety of food and drink vendors to keep you fueled throughout the event.

11. Are there lockers provided at the venue?

Yes. Lockers are available at the venue.

12. Are there any medical facilities at the event in case of emergencies?

We prioritise your safety and well-being. There will be medical facilities and first aid stations on-site, staffed by trained professionals.

13. Is there any ATM at the venue?

No, please proceed to Sunway Pyramid Shopping Mall for ATMs.

Please note that #PFF2024 is a cashless event (does not apply to Sunway Lagoon vendors).

14. What happens if it rains during the event?

Pinkfish Music & Arts Festival 2024 is a rain or shine event! We highly recommend bringing a raincoat or poncho to stay dry if the weather takes a turn.

15. Are there any prohibited items that I’m not allowed to bring?

Banned Items
Cigarette, Selfie sticks/Tripod, Outside Food or Beverage, Professional Cameras or Recording Device, Drones, Air Horns, Blankets or Beach Towels, Umbrellas, Chairs or Coolers, Firework or Explosives, Sharp Items, Laser Pointers, Walkie Talkie, Illegal Drugs or Substances.

Disclaimer:
Pinkfish will not be held responsible for any loss, damage, or confiscation of personal items. Additional prohibited items may or will be added as per the organisers’ discretion without prior notice. All festival goers are subject to security checks.

16. Are professional cameras allowed?

We're excited that you want to capture the event, but unfortunately, we do not permit any form of professional cameras.

17. How can I stay updated on any event announcements?

Stay informed by following our official website and social media channels
Instagram: @pinkfishfestival  
Facebook: Pinkfish Festival

18. I lost my item at the event, where can I retrieve them?

Kindly reach out to us at info@pinkfishfestival.com  / Whatsapp: +6012-2655783. We'll do our best to help you locate your lost item.

18. I’m with a Media House/Company, where can I get my media pass?

Contact us at info@pinkfishfestival.com  / Whatsapp: +6012-2655783 for enquiries.
